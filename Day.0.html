Journey of Code

Day 0 (FEBRUARY 23, 2016)

This is a self-project for myself that displays my progress as I self-learn web-development, hoping to also inspire myself and others to learn to code.

How This All Started

I major in Computer Engineering (a marriage of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I've taken loads of Math courses, Physics, a few programming courses, and even an electrical engineering course. Although I am academic, I've never learned how to make websites (a skill that is highly valued today).

And as a Computer Engineering student, I feel a need to learn everything I can about computers, even web development. Moreover, I have always been looking for a project to make.However, I never had any ideas for a website that I would like to make. No inspiration. Then it hit me.

"Why not make a website that shows people how I learn, including the nitty-gritty details, web-development?"

Not only would I be more motivated by seeing myself make progress, but I would also be showing to people through my self-learning, that despite how LEARNING CODE MIGHT BE INTIMIDATING . . . ANYONE CAN LEARN!





What's Gonna Go Down

Through this project, I will be following a schedule; I will be coding 1 hr everyday, and will be blogging my progress through my website (which is non-existant as I am writing this) regularly, showing what I learned, my challenges, and my progress.
